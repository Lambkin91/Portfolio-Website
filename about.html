About

About me

Hi, I’m Carina, and I‘m a UI/UX designer with a background in UX localization and writing. I have localized several apps and have been working as a UI translator for Facebook and Instagram. I always focus on the best user experience, looking for elegant and clear writing solutions. Also, I have translated developer content and know the basics of web development. Now, I want to combine my UX writing skills and my design skills to be able to accompany the UI/UX design process from start to finish. I also enjoy using my self-taught psychology skills to create satisfying user experiences. If you can't find me at my computer, I'm either reading, writing or enjoying music and art with the help of my synaesthetic brain. When I’m new to a city, I try to go to as many art and arts & crafts museums as I can, seeking inspirations for new designs. My overall goal is to make the web and people's lives more beautiful. Another fun fact about me: I love Asia, especially China! Its language, aesthetics and tasty food always inspire me. I'm also doing freelance translations from Chinese to German.
